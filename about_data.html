About KoFlux Data

This page provides information about the data products available through the KoFlux network. KoFlux integrates data from various regional networks to create harmonized, high-quality datasets for use by the earth system science community.

Our data undergoes rigorous quality assurance and quality control (QA/QC) processes. We provide comprehensive metadata (BADM) files to ensure data users have all the necessary information to interpret and use the data correctly.

Data Products

The primary data product is the KoFlux2025 dataset, which includes data from over 1000 sites globally. We also provide legacy datasets and specialized community products, such as the KoFLUX-CH4 dataset.
