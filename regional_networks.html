KoFLUX Regional Networks

KoFLUX is a part of a wider global network that facilitates the exchange of micrometeorological data, standards, and expertise. These regional networks coordinate efforts to process and harmonize eddy covariance data across various continents and biomes.

Below are the major regional networks collaborating with KoFLUX and FLUXNET:

Collaborating Networks

  • AmeriFlux: The network for the Americas, coordinating data from North and South America.
  • AsiaFlux: A network focused on the monitoring and research of fluxes in Asian ecosystems.
  • BERMS: Boreal Ecosystem Research and Monitoring Sites.
  • EuropeFlux: The European regional network.
  • FluxOz/TFN (Australia): The Australian and New Zealand network.
  • CaribFlux: The network for the Caribbean region.

Each network contributes to the global understanding of climate change impacts on terrestrial ecosystems.
