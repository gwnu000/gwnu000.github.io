Site Summary

Welcome to the Site Information page. Here you can find information for tower sites that participate in the **KoFLUX Dataset**, such as the site general information, a list of site-years of data, and site documentation.

For a comprehensive list of the historical and/or active KoFLUX sites, please refer to the KoFLUX Data Repository or individual **Regional Network** websites. Over the years, there have been more than 50 sites worldwide registered as members of the flux research community.

Please note that not all registered KoFLUX sites are included in the latest KoFLUX Dataset. For data availability, please refer to the official data submission pages or contact us if you find any error in the Site Information page.
